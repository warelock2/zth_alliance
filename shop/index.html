Zero Transaction History Alliance™ Ciphercurrency Cold Storage Wallet™

Partnering with Overt Enigma Cybersecurity Services, we are proud to announce the world's first open source Zero Transaction History Alliance™ Ciphercurrency Cold Storage Hardware Wallet™ device.

This device is currently in the R&D stage and is not yet available for pre-order. Please visit our donations page and please send us some Pirate Chain coins to keep the lights on, while we work hard to bring you world-changing technology, soon to be available for your pre-ordering pleasure. Until then, take a look at the amazing features we're building:

  • Unhackable, untraceable, untaxable, international money
  • Stronger than Fort Knox... in your pocket
  • Runs the exact, official Pirate Chain Treasure Chest binary image downloaded directly from Piratechain.com
  • Fully open source with publicly released build plans 15 days after the last of 50 pre-ordered item ships (This ensures that there are no back doors to harm your financial security)

  • Svelte, sleek, black, 3D printed case with key ring mounting loop
  • Sturdy leatherette snap-close case, protects your touch screen while in your pocket
  • Instantly turns you into a financial ninja
