The First Honest Money Transaction

warelock December 20, 2024

Yesterday, 2024-12-19, @Heiko and @warelock completed the first, in-person, honest money transaction of the ZTH Alliance was completed in San Diego, CA, USA over a steak dinner, beer, scotch, and cigars.

The exchange was between physical silver and Pirate Chain privacy cryptocurrency. All physical silver and physical gold was verified by weight and purity, on-the-spot, instantly, and electronically with a precious metals verifier device and a portable weight scale.

This is just the first of many local transactions of international, honest money with zero public transaction history, coming to a neighborhood near you.
