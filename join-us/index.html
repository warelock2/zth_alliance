Let's finish the job crypto started

In the ancient, offline world when people wanted financial privacy, they would use physical gold and silver. In the modern, online world when people want financial privacy, there's only one choice: Pirate Chain privacy cryptocurrency. The problem is, you can't eat gold and you can't fill your gas/petrol tank with ones and zeroes. True freedom requires a truly free market with prices in honest, international currencies, rejecting dishonest, government-issued currencies. This is the goal of the Zero Transaction History Alliance.

Nobody expects you to quit your "day job". We all use bank accounts. We all swipe our credit cards at the chip and pin machines. All we ask you to consider is this: Stop giving 100% of your value to dishonest currencies like the US Dollar or the British Pound. How about we start at 95%, reserving 5% to create a financial "clean spot" of Liberty in your life?

Only through superior privacy and anonymity technologies combined with the wisdom of the ancient world, will we ever hope to create a world permanently beyond the reach of all banks and all governments.
