Warelock's Dojo

Introduction

While bad ideas can come from anywhere, bad ideas really do deserve to die a public, painful death.

This is a place where we explore diverse topics like: Cryptocurrency, Sound money, Privacy, Political activism, Tax protest, The philosophy of Ayn Rand Objectivism

I call it my "Dojo", because above all, this is a place where we adversarially test ideas against the standards of objective reality and reason in the light of rational self-interest. We also advocate resisting the very human impulse of leaping from speculation to belief without knowledge based in reason and objectively existing facts.

Warelock's Dojo Podcast is video streamed live to Rumble.com and is also available as audio-only content on independent podcast apps, which can be found by searching for "warelock". You can also manually add the RSS feed link to your favorite podcast app. This podcast is proudly sponsored by the Zero Transaction History Alliance.

If you like what you hear on this podcast, please like, subscribe, and donate.
